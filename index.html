🔮 Class Overview

The Warlock is the 8th class in D2R — the first new class added in 25 years, introduced with the Reign of the Warlock expansion. Currently S-Tier in Season 13.

😈

Demon Tree

Summon and bind powerful demons. Field Goatmen and bound enemies as your personal army. Best for beginners — strong and safe.

⚡

Eldritch Tree

Mirror your weapon, hex enemies, and enhance melee combat. Wield a 2-handed weapon in one hand while using a shield. Unique to Warlock.

🔥

Chaos Tree

Dark fire and magic damage. Sigils that alter enemy states on entry. Mix fire and magic paths for devastating AoE destruction.

🗺️ Skill Tree Progression

Where to put your skill points, in what order, and why — for each major build path. You get one skill point per level (plus 1 bonus point from Den of Evil quest × 3 difficulties = 3 free points). Max level is 99, but all builds are complete by level ~85.

🔄 Free respecs: Akara in Act 1 gives one free respec per difficulty (Normal, Nightmare, Hell). Use the Normal respec around level 30–40 to clean up any misplaced early points. Don't waste the Hell respec — save it for when you switch between endgame builds.
Normal
Lvl 1–20
Establish your tank — Summon Goatman
1 Summon Goatman — your very first point, level 1. Your tank is online immediately.
1 Blood Boil — unlock for AoE damage filler while Goatman is fighting.
18 Summon Goatman → 20 — max it. Every point increases HP, damage, and resistances. This is your core until Act 4.

Play style: Let Goatman pull aggro. You stand behind it and toss Blood Boil on packs. Pick up gear from every monster — you need Fire Resistance before Act 2.

Normal
Lvl 20–40
Unlock Bind Demon + supercharge your army
1 Bind Demon — unlock. You can now bind a demon from the world. Try it on a Fallen Shaman first.
1 Death Mark — unlock. Teleports your Bound Demon to any target. Essential for direction control.
1 Hex: Bane — unlock. Apply on elites before your demons attack — amplifies all damage taken.
17 Demonic Mastery → 20 — core passive. Every point increases your entire demon army's damage, speed, and HP. Massive value.

Key milestone: Around level 35 in Act 4 Normal, bind Hephasto the Armorer (River of Flame). He becomes your permanent Bound Demon — massive single-target DPS. Use Death Mark to send him into fights.

Cap your resistances now. Nightmare carries -40% resist penalty. Make Ancients' Pledge shield (Ral + Ort + Tal from Hellforge quest) and Stealth armor if you haven't already.

📈 Leveling Guide (1 → 75)

Level 75 is the breakpoint for most endgame gear and builds. Here's the fastest path.

1–15

Act 1 — Normal

Put first 3 points into Summon Goatman. Rush through Den of Evil and Cold Plains. Your Goatman tanks everything — stay behind it.

15–25

Act 2–3 — Normal

Max Summon Goatman. Add 1 point to Bind Demon. Start putting points into Demonic Mastery. Maggot Lair and Travincal are great XP.

25–40

Act 4–5 — Normal

Keep maxing Demonic Mastery. Rush Chaos Sanctuary. Start adding Death Mark for Bound Demon control. Baal runs for the final levels.

40–55

Nightmare Difficulty

Rush through with your demon army. Nightmare Andariel and Mephisto give great XP and drops. Bind stronger demons as you progress.

55–75

Hell Difficulty

Max Blood Oath and Sigil: Lethargy. Cap your resistances before entering Hell. Farm Nightmare Baal for levels 65-75 if Hell feels too hard.

75+

Endgame Setup

Transition to your chosen endgame build. Get Enigma for teleport. Respec using Akara in Act 1 (one free per difficulty) if needed.

💡 Starter Tip: The Demon Summoner build requires almost no gear to function. It's the safest ladder starter — your demons carry you even with bad equipment. Start here, farm gear, then respec into Mirrored Blades or Echoing Strike.

💎 Runes — What They Are & How to Use Them

Runes are special items that look like gems but do something completely different. You put them into socketed items (items with empty holes). Put the right runes in the right order and you make a Runeword — one of the most powerful items in the game.

🔰 Beginner basics: Runes drop from monsters and chests. The higher the rune, the rarer it is. You can combine 3 identical low runes in the Horadric Cube to get one higher rune. El + El + El = Eld, and so on up the chain.

How to Socket an Item

  • Find or buy an item with empty sockets (shown as grey circles)
  • Open your inventory. Click the rune, then click the socketed item
  • The rune is now permanently inserted — you cannot remove it!
  • To get sockets on an item: Larzuk in Act 5 adds sockets (1 quest reward per difficulty)
  • Horadric Cube recipe also works: item + Hel rune + Scroll of Town Portal = removes all runes (destroys them)

How Runewords Work

  • Insert runes in exact order shown in the recipe
  • The item must be the exact type listed (e.g. Body Armor, Shield, Sword)
  • The item must be grey/white (not magic/rare/set/unique — those won't work)
  • It must have the exact number of sockets required — not more, not less
  • When done correctly, the item glows gold and shows the runeword name

🔱 Runewords — Full Recipes & Explanations

These are the runewords every Warlock needs to know. Listed from easiest to make (early game) to hardest (endgame).

🟢 Early Game

Stealth

2-socket Body Armor

Tal → Eth
  • +25% Faster Run/Walk
  • +25% Faster Cast Rate
  • +25% Faster Hit Recovery
  • Regenerate Mana 15%

Why: Your first real armor. The movement and cast speed make the early game much easier. Make this as soon as possible — you can find Tal and Eth from any normal monster.

🟢 Early Game

Rhyme

2-socket Shield

Shael → Eth
  • +25% All Resistances
  • Cannot Be Frozen
  • 20% Increased Chance of Blocking
  • +40 to Mana

Why: The "Cannot Be Frozen" is huge — frozen monsters slow your attack and cast speed. Use this until you get Spirit. Very easy runes to find.

🟢 Early Game

Ancients' Pledge

3-socket Shield

Ral → Ort → Tal
  • +43% Fire Resistance
  • +48% Lightning Resistance
  • +48% Poison Resistance
  • +48% Cold Resistance
  • 10% Damage Goes to Mana

Why: The easiest way to cap your resistances for Hell difficulty. You get Ral, Ort, Tal as guaranteed drops from the Hellforge quest in Act 4.

🟡 Mid Game

Insight Merc Weapon

4-socket Polearm (for your Mercenary)

Ral → Tir → Tal → Sol
  • Level 12–17 Meditation Aura
  • Massive Mana Regeneration (from Meditation)
  • +35% Faster Cast Rate
  • +200-260% Enhanced Damage

Why: The Meditation aura means you never run out of mana again. Give this to your Act 2 mercenary. Game-changing for spell-heavy builds like Echoing Strike. Use an Eth polearm for best results.

🟡 Mid Game

Spirit

4-socket Sword or 4-socket Shield

Tal → Thul → Ort → Amn
  • +2 to All Skills
  • +25-35% Faster Cast Rate
  • +89-112 to Mana
  • +3-8 to Magic Absorb
  • Adds 1-50 Lightning Damage

Why: Best value runeword in the entire game. +2 skills on a shield AND a sword means +4 skills total from two Spirits. Farm a 4-socket sword from Act 1 Nightmare Diablo or Nightmare Mephisto. Runes are all low-level and easy to find.

🟡 Mid Game

Fortitude Merc Armor

4-socket Body Armor (for your Mercenary)

El → Sol → Dol → Lo
  • +300% Enhanced Damage
  • +200% Enhanced Defense
  • +15 Defense per Level
  • All Resistances +25-30
  • 20% Deadly Strike

Why: The standard merc armor. Massive defense keeps your mercenary alive. Can also go on your own armor if you don't have Enigma yet. Requires Lo rune — a high rune, worth farming Travincal for.

🔴 High End

Chains of Honor

4-socket Body Armor

Dol → Um → Ber → Ist
  • +2 to All Skills
  • +70% Damage to Demons
  • +200% Damage to Undead
  • +65 Resistances (all elements)
  • 8% Life Stolen per Hit

Why: Alternative to Enigma when you don't need teleport. The +65 to all resistances alone makes Hell trivial. +70% damage to demons is huge for Warlock builds fighting in Chaos Sanctuary. Requires a Ber rune (very rare).

🔴 High End

Beast

5-socket Axe / Scepter / Hammer

Ber → Tir → Um → Mal → Lum
  • Level 9 Fanaticism Aura (when equipped)
  • +40% Increased Attack Speed
  • +240-270% Enhanced Damage
  • +3 to Werebear / Werewolf
  • Level 13 Summon Grizzly (5 charges)

Why: The Fanaticism aura boosts your entire demon army's attack speed and damage — it stacks with everything. This is the #1 weapon for Demon Summoner builds. You equip this yourself (remember, Warlock uses 2H weapons in 1 hand!). Requires a Ber rune.

👑 Best in Slot

Enigma — The Most Important Item in the Game

3-socket Body Armor (Mage Plate recommended)

Jah → Ith → Ber
  • ⚡ Teleport — you can teleport anywhere on screen instantly
  • +2 to All Skills
  • +45% Faster Run/Walk
  • +750-775 Defense
  • Increase Maximum Life 5%
  • +14% Life after Each Kill

Why Teleport changes everything: Normally only Sorceresses can teleport. Enigma gives any class the ability to teleport. This means you can skip through entire maps in seconds, teleport to bosses, dodge attacks, and farm 10× faster. Every endgame Warlock needs this. It requires Jah + Ber, two of the rarest runes — but it's worth farming for months. Use a low-strength armor (Mage Plate, Archon Plate) so you can equip it easily.

🔑 Priority #1 farming goal. Jah drops in Hell difficulty. Best sources: Travincal, Chaos Sanctuary, Countess (Hell), LK Superchests.
🔴 High End

Pride Merc Weapon

4-socket Polearm (for your Mercenary)

Cham → Sur → Io → Lo
  • Level 16-20 Concentration Aura
  • Concentration adds +200% Enhanced Damage to your demons
  • +260-300% Bonus to Attack Rating
  • +1 to Teleport (charges)

Why: The Concentration aura from your mercenary stacks with your own Fanaticism (from Beast), creating absurd demon damage. This is the endgame merc weapon for Demon Summoner builds once you have Beast and Enigma already.

🎒 Key Gear — What It Is & Why You Want It

These are the unique items mentioned in the build guides. Here's what each one actually does in plain language.

Helm

Harlequin Crest "Shako"

  • +2 to All Skills
  • +1-148 to Life (scales with level)
  • +1-148 to Mana (scales with level)
  • Damage Reduced by 10%
  • 50% Better Chance of Getting Magic Items

The best all-around helm in the game. +2 skills, life, mana, and magic find in one item. Farm Nightmare Andariel for a chance at this drop.

Amulet

Mara's Kaleidoscope

  • +2 to All Skills
  • All Resistances +20-30
  • +5 to All Attributes

Simple and perfect. +2 skills on an amulet slot with resistance coverage. The +skills and resistances are both things Warlocks desperately need.

Belt

Arachnid Mesh

  • +1 to All Skills
  • +20% Faster Cast Rate
  • Slows Target by 10%
  • +90-120% Enhanced Defense

The only belt that gives both +skills and Faster Cast Rate. Every build that uses any spells wants this. Drop source: Hell Mephisto, Hell Andariel, Pindleskin.

Ring

Stone of Jordan "SoJ"

  • +1 to All Skills
  • +20 to Mana
  • Increase Maximum Mana 25%
  • +1-12 Lightning Damage

The best ring in the game for casters. +1 skill per ring means you wear two of them for +2 skills just from rings. Also triggers Uber Diablo to spawn when sold to a vendor in large quantities.

Gloves

Dracul's Grasp

  • 5-10% Life Stolen per Hit
  • 25% Chance of Open Wounds
  • 7-10% Life after Each Kill
  • Level 10 Life Tap on Striking (25% chance)

The Life Tap proc is what matters — when it triggers, it causes 50% of all damage dealt to be leeched as life. Your demons and you heal massively during combat. Essential for Demon Summoner.

Boots

War Traveler

  • +25% Faster Run/Walk
  • Adds 15-25 Damage
  • +10 to Vitality
  • +10 to Strength
  • 40-50% Better Chance of Getting Magic Items

Great all-around boots with solid magic find. The movement speed and stat bonuses help throughout the game. Good budget choice that stays relevant into endgame.

Helm

Andariel's Visage

  • +2 to All Skills
  • +10% Increased Attack Speed
  • 8-10% Life Stolen per Hit
  • +25-30% to Poison Resistance
  • 15% Chance to Cast Level 15 Poison Nova on Striking

The best merc helm. Huge life leech keeps your mercenary alive through anything. The +2 skills and attack speed make your merc hit harder and faster. Farm it from Andariel herself.

Cube

Horadric Cube Essential Tool

  • Found in Act 2 (Halls of the Dead, Level 3)
  • Transmute 3 identical runes → 1 higher rune
  • Upgrade set/unique items to higher base type
  • Craft items with specific affixes
  • Remove sockets (destroys the runes inside)

Not a gear piece — it's a tool. You MUST have the Horadric Cube to upgrade runes. Keep it in your inventory at all times. It takes up 2×2 inventory space but it's worth it.

Mercenary

Act 2 Offensive Mercenary

  • Hire from Greiz in Act 2 (Nightmare difficulty for endgame)
  • Has Might Aura — increases physical damage of all nearby allies
  • Tanky and deals decent damage
  • Equip: Pride/Insight (weapon) + Fortitude/CoH (armor) + Andariel's (helm)

The standard mercenary for Warlock. The Might aura directly amplifies your demon army's physical damage. Always hire from Nightmare difficulty — Normal mercenaries have lower stats.

📊 Resistance cap: You need 75% in every element (Fire, Cold, Lightning, Poison) when playing Hell difficulty. Below 75% you take massive damage. Ancients' Pledge shield + Smoke armor gets you capped cheaply. Check your resistances in the character screen (press C) — anything under 75 needs fixing before you enter Hell.

💰 Farming Spots

Where to grind Runes, Unique items, and gear for your Warlock.

🧙 The Countess

  • Best dedicated rune farming loop in the game
  • Location: Black Marsh → Forgotten Tower Level 5 (Act 1)
  • Normal: up to Ral · NM: up to Ist · Hell: up to Lo
  • Drops 1–3 runes per kill from her own special table
  • Farm NM for Spirit/Insight runes, Hell for Gul/Vex/Lo

🏛️ The Pit

  • Best early magic find spot
  • Location: Tamoe Highland (Act 1, Hell)
  • No immunities — everything is killable
  • High density, great unique drops

👸 Andariel

  • Act 1 boss — fast to kill
  • Drops Harlequin Crest, Mara's, Hotspurs
  • Level 25 area — easy with Goatman tank
  • Farm on Nightmare for low-level uniques

🐮 Secret Cow Level

  • Portal: Combine Wirt's Leg + Tome of Town Portal
  • Dense packs — excellent XP and drops
  • Hell Cows require strong resistances
  • Great for Sigil: Death + Cleave Warlock

🔥 Chaos Sanctuary

  • Act 4 — Diablo's domain
  • Dense elite packs + Diablo boss kill
  • Drops high runes and top tier uniques
  • Bind a Venom Lord for insane bound demon DPS

🌊 River of Flame

  • Just before Chaos Sanctuary (Act 4, Hell)
  • Good pack density, elite packs
  • Combine with Chaos Sanctuary run
  • Hephasto the Armorer spawns here — bind him!

💎 Travincal

  • High Council — 3 unique monsters guaranteed
  • Best Beli and Rune farming in Act 3
  • Drops High Runes on Hell difficulty
  • Bind a Council member for free DPS

👾 Eldritch & Shenk

  • Act 5, Hell — Frigid Highlands area
  • Fast clears, guaranteed boss kills
  • Shenk drops can include top uniques
  • Good warm-up run before Baal

💡 Pro Tips

🔗

Bind the Right Demon

Hephasto the Armorer (River of Flame) with a Cursed affix is the best Bound Demon. Cursed applies Amplify Damage — doubles your demon's damage output.

🛡️

Unique Mechanic — Use It

Only Warlocks can wield a 2-handed weapon in one hand with a shield in the other. Always use a shield — never go two-handed.

📖

Exclusive Runeword Bases

Warlocks have access to exclusive "shield book" types and special daggers as runeword bases. Check for Warlock-specific runewords before using generic ones.

⚡

Cap Resistances First

75% in all resistances is mandatory for Hell difficulty. Use Smoke, Rhyme, or Ancients' Pledge before worrying about damage. Dead Warlocks do zero damage.

🔄

Free Respec

Talk to Akara in Act 1 for one free respec per difficulty (Normal, Nightmare, Hell). Use the Normal respec to transition from leveling build to endgame build around level 60.

🧿

Sigil Placement Matters

Cast Sigil: Death just before enemies enter a room — they walk right into it. Cast Sigil: Lethargy on bosses before your demon attacks to shred their Physical Damage Reduction.

🧪

Get Enigma Early

The Enigma runeword gives Teleport. This changes everything — you can skip through levels instantly. Farm Jah + Ith + Ber runes as a top priority.

🐐

Goatman Is Your Best Friend

Summon Goatman should always be up. Re-summon if it dies. With max Demonic Mastery it hits like a truck and has enormous health. Keep it between you and enemies.
